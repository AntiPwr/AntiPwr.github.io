# Preacher's Vassal

**Preacher's Vassal** is a flatland region east of Luin's Point and the East Gate of [[Southcatch]], [[Scender]]. This land is reserved by local saltwater river fish farms that carry a rich history in Sancta dating back to the founding of Scender during the [[Cel Blindbirth Line]].
